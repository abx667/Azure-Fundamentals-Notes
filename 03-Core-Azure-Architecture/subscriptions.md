  # 💳 Subscriptions
 
An Azure subscription is a logical unit used for billing and access management.
 
---
 
## 🔑 Key Points
 
- Tracks usage and billing  
- Controls access to resources  
- Acts as a boundary for resource deployment  
 
---
 
## 📌 Example
 
A company may have separate subscriptions for:
- Development  
- Testing  
- Production  
 
---
 
## 🎯 Important
 
- Each subscription is linked to a billing account  
- Helps in cost management  
 
---
 
## 🧠 Quick Revision
 
- Subscription = billing + access boundary  
- Controls who can use resources
