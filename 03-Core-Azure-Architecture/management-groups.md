# 🏢 Management Groups
 
Management groups help organize multiple Azure subscriptions.
 
---
 
## 🔑 Key Points
 
- Used to manage access, policies, and compliance across subscriptions  
- Can group multiple subscriptions together  
- Applies governance at scale  
 
---
 
## 📌 Example
 
An organization can group subscriptions by:
- Department  
- Environment (Dev, Test, Prod)  
 
---
 
## 🎯 Important
 
- Policies applied at management group level apply to all subscriptions under it  
- Helps in centralized management  
 
---
 
## 🧠 Quick Revision
 
- Management Group = group of subscriptions  
- Used for governance and policy control
